② Environment
Where is price coming FROM and where is it going TO? Internal = Imbalance/Gap or Old High/Low. External = Old High/Low beyond prior range.
③ Bias
Read the previous candle's OHLC pattern on your chosen timeframe. A tap of internal liquidity can flip or confirm the bias — monitor lower TFs for entry confirmation.
④ Confirmation
A+ Setup = Sweep of previous candle/session HIGH + Internal Liquidity Gap supporting the bias.
